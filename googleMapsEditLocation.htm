Configuring Google Map View
  Router Name: 192.168.9.209
  Location Name: Show on Map
Help : Please choose any one of the two options to select a location on the map
  • Click on the location to place the device on the map. Use the controls on the top left to navigate or zoom.
  • To change the location, click on the image. It will vanish and you can select a new location.
  • Enter the location in the 'Location Name' field and hit "Save location"
  • Enter the location in the "Location Name" and click on "Show on Map". The image will be placed on the location specified.
  • Click on "Save Location"
